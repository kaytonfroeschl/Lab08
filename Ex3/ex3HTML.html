Hannah Montana

Biography

Miley Stewart is a fourteen-year-old middle school student who appears to live a normal life as a teenager. Unbeknown to the general public, Miley holds a secret identity as international pop singer Hannah Montana; an alias she fabricated in order to live a private life away from the public spotlight. The teenager uses a blonde wig when appearing as Hannah to conceal her true identity. Miley's best friend Lilly Truscott uncovers the truth about Miley's hidden alter ego after her unsuccessful attempts to hide the celebrity identity. Throughout the first season, Lilly adopts the alias of Lola in order to assist in protecting Miley's secret. Miley's father, Robby Ray Stewart, was a famous country music singer before retiring from the industry to focus on raising his two children, Miley and her older brother Jackson, after the death of his wife. At the commencement of the series, the family have moved from Tennessee to Malibu, California to allow Miley the opportunities associated with her musical career, with Robby Ray working as the singer's manager.
